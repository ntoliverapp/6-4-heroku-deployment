Water Signs:

Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio: As a water sign, you feel empathy for others. You lead with emotion and spirituality.
Activism Style: Consider movements where you can hold space and care for others. Your sixth sense will help you know how to best advocate for them.

Fire Signs:

Aries, Leo, Sagittarius: Your fiery personality can light up a room and spark the energy for change in others!
Activism Style: Consider leadership positions where you can take direct action. You can engage the crowd at any event.

Earth Signs:

Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn: Your earth energy keeps you stable and grounded during tough situations. You are the rock of your friend group, they know they can always count on you.
Activism Style: You can do well as a mediator of tense situations, keeping everyone calm and at ease. You should also consider handling the logistics, ensuring all the movement supplies get to everyone who needs them.

Air Signs:

Aquarius, Gemini, Libra: As an air sign, you have an altruistic approach to the world. You are passionate about justice and equality. You love to communicate your thoughts and ideas.
Activism Style: Consider movements where you can care for others. Your sixth sense will help you know how to best advocate for them.
